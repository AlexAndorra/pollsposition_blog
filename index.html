Forecasting French Elections with Bayesian Statistics

This website is a collection of tutorials detailing the models powering the electoral forecasts displayed at PollsPosition.com

The models are [open-sourced](https://github.com/AlexAndorra/pollsposition_models) and stand on the shoulders of giants of the [Python](https://www.python.org/) data stack: [PyMC3](https://docs.pymc.io/) for state-of-the-art MCMC algorithms, [ArviZ](https://arviz-devs.github.io/arviz/) and [Bokeh](https://docs.bokeh.org/en/latest/) for visualizations, and [Pandas](https://pandas.pydata.org/) for data cleaning. We warmly thank all the developers who give their time to develop these free, open-source and high quality scientific tools -- just like The Avengers, they really are true heroes.
